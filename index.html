About PremsFoundation

Established in 2010, we have been working tirelessly to uplift underprivileged communities across multiple regions.

Our Mission & Vision

We envision a world where every individual has access to basic necessities, education, and opportunities to thrive. Our mission is to empower communities through sustainable development programs.

At PremsFoundation, we believe in transparency, accountability, and community participation. Every project is designed with input from local communities to ensure maximum impact.

Our Programs

We run multiple initiatives focused on education, healthcare, women empowerment, and environmental sustainability.

Education for All

Providing quality education to children in remote areas, building schools, and offering scholarship programs.

Healthcare Initiatives

Mobile medical camps, health awareness campaigns, and support for maternal and child health.

Women Empowerment

Skill development programs, micro-finance support, and legal awareness for women's rights.
